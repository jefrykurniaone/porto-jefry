<objective>
Fix the low-contrast muted text (UI-05) by overriding the SGDS token `--sgds-color-muted`
in `globals.css` for both the light (`:root`) and dark (`:root.sgds-night-theme`) scopes.
The token currently resolves to `#c6c6c6` (day) / `#3b3b3b` (night) — ~1.6:1 on the page
background, far below WCAG AA 4.5:1 — which is the root cause of the hard-to-read gray text
the owner flagged (hero job title, hero subtitle, section meta).

Purpose: Make all muted text legible without touching any component.
Output: An updated `src/app/globals.css` with AA-passing `--sgds-color-muted` values.
</objective>
